  • LLM-Powered Document OCR System

    An intelligent document processing system that uses Large Language Models (LLMs) to perform OCR and structured data extraction from vendor and compliance documents, including government-issued IDs (e.g., national ID cards and driver's licenses), approval forms, invoices, and tax documents. The system automatically validates extracted data to reduce manual review and submission errors, shortens payment disbursement cycles by minimizing rejections, and supports fraud detection workflows.

    Technologies: Python, FastAPI, GCP Cloud Functions, Instructor, GCP Datastore, Docker

  • LLM Customer Service Chatbot

    An AI-powered customer service chatbot built with Large Language Models (LLMs) that acts as the first point of contact for customer enquiries on messaging platforms such as WhatsApp, handling reservations, FAQs, and complaints. The system reduces customer service workload, improves response times, and allows human agents to focus on complex or high-value interactions.

    Technologies: Python, n8n, Chatwoot, Instructor, PostgreSQL, GCP Compute Engine, Docker
